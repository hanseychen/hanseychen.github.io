I am a research scientist at Facebook working on building scalable, efficient, and dependable infrastructures for training machine learning systems. Prior to joining Facebook, I was a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University, advised by Dr. Feng Qin. My research mainly focused on combating bugs in cloud systems, but I'm generally interested in any problems related to building, or helping developers build dependable software systems.
